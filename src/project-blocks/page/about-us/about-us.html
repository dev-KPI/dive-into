DIVE INTO COMMUNITY

A community of people interested in IT wants to find like-minded people and realize their fantasies. Community members are involved in the implementation of various projects using modern technologies. It is a platform for everyone who wants to learn, create and communicate.

The community was founded by students of the Institute of Atomic and Thermal Energy of the National Technical University of Ukraine "Ihor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute".
